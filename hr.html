Our Mission

Samadhan is a smart platform where citizens can report civic issues such as garbage, water leakage, streetlight failures, or unhygienic conditions with just one click. With photo + location-based reporting, issues are sent directly to Nagar Nigam, ensuring quick action, accountability, and a cleaner city.
